Who we are

Greenpanion is both a web and mobile application created by seven hard-working students, motivated to make the world a better place. Thinking about all the ways we could help the environment, we decided to help build a more sustainable world through recycling. It is the process by which waste products are broken down to their basic materials and remade into new products. This leads to less need for raw materials, which means that the energy and time invested in extracting the raw materials would only need to happen once and those materials would constantly be cycled and recycled, having a positive impact on the environment.
