Here to help you to understand our process

As a Greenpanion app user you can contribute to saving the planet by going to some of our recycling centers which can be seen on the attached map below. Because we want to give people a boost that motivates them to much more dedication and ambition, we decided to reward you accordingly. This depends on how much and how you recycle, but that's why we're here to help!

How do you proceed ?

  • Create an user account on our website or on the Android App for a total experience you won't regret.
  • Once you have logged in on your phone app, navigate to the section "How to recycle?" "Scan Code"
  • All you have to do is put your recycled items into a machine in our recycling centers and wait for the generation of the QR code that you are going to scan with the app.
  • Depending on the amount and recycled material you will receive a certain number of points (XP) and you can level up. For every 500XP accumulated you advance by one level.
  • The more you advance in the level, the prizes become more and more attractive. That's why you don't have to waste your time!
